About Cavan Town

Cavan (An Cabhán, meaning 'The Hollow') is the county town of County Cavan in Ireland. The town lies in Ulster, near the border with County Fermanagh in Northern Ireland. The town is on the main N3 road that links Dublin (to the south) with Enniskillen, Ballyshannon and Donegal Town (to the north).

Things to do.

Every Saturday, check out the enticing smells in Cavan Town as market stalls sell organic meat, fresh fish, homebaked treats and local cheeses. Go bowling with friends, play a round of golf or plan your trip around the annual heritage week to discover the town's rich history. In the evening, enjoy charming restaurants, cosy pubs and buzzing nightlife.
