THE PURGE

The mayor of Cavan watched the movie "The purge" and mistook it as a documentary. But this time it's with a twist! Instead of 1 day a year without laws, Cavan only has 1 day a year with laws! Fun for the whole family!

Pillow screaming competition!

Cavan has a long history of utter frustation, and what a better way to relieve it than some traditional pillow screaming? Prizes go for "Most muffled", "Most audible" and also "Most concerning" so prizes are attainable for people of all skill levels!

UFC - Ultimate Fighting Cavan

Not to be confused with the knock off "Ultimate Fighting Championship". Ultimate fighting Cavan is the world's only fighting tournament that is legally binding in its outcome. Had your planning permission rejected? Well, in Cavan you can fight for it! (Must be over 8 years old to participate)

Not to be confused with a festival full of small hot air balloons. Oh no, our festival is full of a single normal sized hot air balloon. Officially recognized by guinness world records as "The most unremarkable festival in the world". Not to be missed!
